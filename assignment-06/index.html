Easy Pancake Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 3 1/2 tsp. baking powder
  • 1 tbsp. sugar
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • 1 1/4 tbsp. melted butter
  • 1 egg

Directions:

  1. Sift flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt together in a large bowl.
  2. Make a well in the center and add milk, melted butter, and egg; mix until smooth
  3. Pour or scoop 1/4 cup of batter onto a greased griddle, cook at medium heat until bubbles form and the edges are dry, about 2 to 3 minutes.
  4. Flip and cook until browned on the other side. Repeat with remaining batter.
